ACME CORP EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK
Version 2.3 - Updated Jan 2024

1. VACATION AND TIME OFF (PTO)
All full-time employees at Acme Corp are granted 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) per calendar year. New employees accrue PTO at a rate of 1.66 days per month during their first year. PTO does not roll over to the next year. If you do not use your 20 days by December 31st, they expire.

2. WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Acme Corp operates on a hybrid schedule. Employees are required to be in the office on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays. Mondays and Fridays are designated as Work From Home (WFH) days. You may work from a different state or country for up to 4 weeks a year, provided you notify your manager 2 weeks in advance.

3. EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
Employees working remotely are entitled to a one-time $500 stipend to set up their home office (desks, ergonomic chairs, monitors). 
Additionally, employees can expense up to $60 per month for home internet/wi-fi.
All expenses must be submitted through the Expensify portal by the 5th of the following month. Receipts are required for any purchase over $25.

4. SICK LEAVE
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. A doctor's note is required for any sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive working days.

5. HOLIDAYS
Acme Corp observes the following paid holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (Thursday and Friday)
- Christmas Eve and Christmas Day
